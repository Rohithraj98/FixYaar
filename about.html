About FIXYAAR

At FIXYAAR, we believe vehicle servicing should be simple, reliable, and convenient. Our mission is to bring professional mechanics right to your doorstep, whether it’s a two-wheeler or a four-wheeler. We partner with certified garages to ensure high-quality service with genuine parts, so you can trust your vehicle is in safe hands.

Our Story

FIXYAAR was founded to solve the common challenges vehicle owners face: unreliable garages, unclear pricing, and wasted time. From humble beginnings, we’ve grown into a trusted platform connecting vehicle owners with experienced mechanics, making doorstep service accessible to everyone in Hyderabad.

Our Values

Transparency: Clear pricing, no hidden costs.
Reliability: Certified mechanics at your service.
Convenience: Doorstep repairs for 2W & 4W vehicles.
Quality: Genuine parts and guaranteed workmanship.
